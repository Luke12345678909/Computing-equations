The calculate function if you put in one argument it will return it back.
If you put two arguments it will say if they are equal.
Functions:
add takes two numbers and adds them.
Example:add(5,3)=8
subtract takes two numbers and subtracts them
Example:subtract(9,5)=4
multiply takes two numbers and multiplies them
Example:multiply(5,5)=25
divide takes two numbers and divides them
Example:divide(10,5)=2
